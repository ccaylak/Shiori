What’s New:

New Features
- You can now open details from your library by tapping a cover while editing an entry
- Choose how anime progress is displayed in the library (episode only or with additional estimated remaining time)
- Choose how manga progress is tracked (chapters, volumes, or both)
- Added an Advanced Mode toggle for simpler tracking (progress, episodes, rating)

Reworks
- Title language setting moved to Settings (instead of the Library)
- Removed Portuguese as a language (the app is changing too frequently right now)
- Library and search filters now use matching icons, so you can easily see what’s active
- Updated the Library PillPicker with a “liquid glass” style
- Updated the appearance of the Season view
- Genres are now grouped more clearly in the Genre view

Bug Fixes
- Fixed “Load More” button in the results view
- Fixed glitching share button
